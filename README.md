## 🧬 About me

I'm an **Android Reverse Engineer & Malware Analyst** with a background in Android development, mobile security, and tooling.

Professionally, my work is focused on Android: APK analysis, DEX bytecode, malware reversing, instrumentation, deobfuscation, and understanding how apps behave under the hood.

Outside of work, I like building side projects around my hobbies, games/apps modding, visual experiments, automation, and small utilities that solve annoying everyday problems.

> If I hit the same problem twice, there is a good chance I will try to automate it.
